Bank Term Deposit Analysis

On this page, you'll find an interactive R Shiny dashboard built using the Bank Term Deposit Subscription Prediction Dataset.

Here's the link for the Dashboard : Bank Term Deposit Analysis Dashboard

Happy to present this fresh new dashboard. The first version is available and we can analyze the distribution of jobs and the impact of education and age on wheter the clients subscribe to a bank term deposit.

Get the cool and soon to come features update !

  • A new chart !
    Campaign Performance Trend     : This chart will analyze the performance of the campaign over the months. We will identify seasonal patterns, peak performance months and periods of lower engagement.
  • A new tab !
    Binary Classification     : A new tab that will predict whether a client will subscribe to a bank term deposit.
